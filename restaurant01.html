Chyorisangkyung

Chyorisangkyung located in Seongsu-dong near Seoul Forest, is celebrated for its traditional and innovative approach to Korean cuisine, particularly its specialty in pot rice dishes.

Their menu includes a variety of pot rice options, like the notably delicate and flavorful Salmon Pot Rice ,Steak Pot Rice etc.
