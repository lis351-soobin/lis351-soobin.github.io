Places in Seoul

Seoul, a city that harmoniously blends ancient history with cutting-edge modernity, offers a myriad of attractions for visitors.

Landmarks like Gyeongbokgung Palace and Bukchon Hanok Village allow a glimpse into Korea's rich heritage, while the futuristic skyline led by N Seoul Tower and the bustling streets of Myeongdong shopping district reflect its modern advancements.

For nature enthusiasts, the Han River and Seoullo 7017 provide serene escapes within the urban landscape, offering leisure activities and scenic walks amidst the city's vibrant life.
